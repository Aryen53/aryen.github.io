WELCOME TO ARYEN SCHOOLS

ARYEN SCHOOLS was founded in 1957 as a Technical Training School by the then colonial Government. It was converted into ARYEN Technical and Trade School in 1958, ARYEN Technical School in 1967, ARYEN SCHOOLS Collage in 2013 and chartered to ARYEN SCHOOLS in October 2016.
